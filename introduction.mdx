## Overview

Bloqade Circuit is a core component of QuEra's Neutral Atom SDK, providing powerful embedded domain-specific languages (eDSLs) for programming neutral atom quantum computers. It abstracts away the complexities of neutral atom quantum computing, allowing you to focus on developing quantum algorithms and compilation strategies.

## Circuit Dialects

Bloqade Circuit provides multiple embedded domain-specific languages, each designed for specific quantum computing workflows:

- **QASM2**: Industry-standard OpenQASM 2.0 dialect with extensions for parallel gates and noise
- **Squin**: Python-native quantum circuit DSL with rich gate libraries and noise channels
- **STIM**: Stabilizer circuit simulation optimized for quantum error correction
- **Native**: Direct neutral atom gate operations for hardware-level control
- **Gemini**: Logical operations for fault-tolerant quantum computing
